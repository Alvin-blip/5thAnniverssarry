About Us

In the halls of Asian Institute of Computer Studies, our love story began with a simple Rubik's Cube. Just like this puzzle, our relationship has seen its fair share of challenges and complexities. We've argued, puzzled, and solved problems, but through it all, we never tire of finding solutions together. Our love is a beautiful journey of growth and understanding, where we face each challenge with unwavering determination. Like the colors of the Rubik's Cube coming together in harmony, our hearts intertwine, creating a love that knows no bounds. With each passing day, our bond grows stronger, and our love becomes an ever-evolving masterpiece.

Our first met

Her seatmate playfully challenged her, promising snacks if she could solve the Rubik's cube. Curiosity got the better of her, and she borrowed my Rubik's cube. As she attempted to solve it, I couldn't help but observe her struggle. There was something intriguing about the way she approached the challenge.

Eventually, she turned to me, seeking help to conquer the puzzle. With a smile, I gladly assisted her, guiding her through the twists and turns. In that moment, as I watched her determination and charm, something within me shifted.

I had never been one to pay much attention to girls, but she was different. Her presence stood out, and I couldn't deny that I found her undeniably cute. From that day on, my eyes were drawn to her, and our shared connection over the Rubik's cube was the beginning of an unforgettable journey.
